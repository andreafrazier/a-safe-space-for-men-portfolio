Usage Instructions:

  • Logo Embedded: Your handshake logo is centered in each QR code with a white border for maximum scanability
  • Download: Click the button under each QR code to download as PNG
  • Print Quality: These QR codes are 512x512px, suitable for print up to 3-4 inches
  • Tracking: Each QR code has a unique source parameter to track where donations come from
  • Test First: Scan with your phone before printing to ensure they work
  • Placement Ideas: Flyers, business cards, event posters, social media posts, email signatures

Note: QR codes with logos require higher error correction to remain scannable. These are optimized for reliability.
